Privacy-safe analytics are off by default. If you turn them on later, they stay local to your browser and avoid page text and raw URLs.

What is collected

If enabled, aggregate counts only: applies, resets, mode usage, and counts of unique sites and pages using local salted hashes.

What is not collected

No page content, no selected text, no raw URLs, and no analytics sent to a remote server.
